FOUNDER

About Founder

Sakshi has worked in the event industry for over 5 years in Delhi & Mumbai. She founded Resonate Events Private Limited in 2016. Throughout her career, she has been actively involved in the full spectrum of event management services including strategy creation, curation, brand management, promosions,event management & PR. Apart from event managemnt, Sakshi is an Actor & Writer and has acted in several shorts films & TV commercials after acquiring a degree in Acting from Whistling Woods International.

Founder's Message

Event management is an art that comes very naturally to me. I believe that my life's purpose involves serving others which led me to the idea of organising a festival that is an amalgamation of the best of everything and create a world of Indulgence that offers an escape from usual city life, a weekend that gets me high on Happiness.
My vision with Indulge is to re-define the quality of Events/Festivals and explore opportunities to create something extra-ordinary.

-SAKSHI KAKKAR
